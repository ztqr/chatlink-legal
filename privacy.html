ChatLink Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information Collected

ChatLink may collect:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord usernames
  • Discord server IDs
  • Message content sent through ChatLink
  • Reports submitted by users
  • Moderation records

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

  • Provide cross-server communication
  • Process reports
  • Prevent abuse
  • Maintain bot security

3. Message Data

ChatLink may temporarily store recent messages for moderation and report evidence.

Messages are not sold or shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage

Data may be stored securely in bot databases or files.

Stored information may include:

  • Reports
  • Blocked users
  • Blocked servers

5. Data Removal

Users may request removal of stored data by contacting the ChatLink Support Server.

6. Third Parties

ChatLink operates on Discord and follows Discord's policies. Discord may separately process information according to their own Privacy Policy.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated when needed.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the ChatLink Support Server.
